About

Taylor Gehrts has been working in technology in one way or another since 2015 when he co-founded Mordax Systems , a small boutique synthesizer company. Since then he has managed restaurants and bars, helping with internal systems and managing people. He is currently enrolled in a web development bootcamp through the University of Oregon. His interests include making electronic music, cooking and taking care of his two cats.
